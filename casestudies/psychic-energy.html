Psychic Energy Helps Find a Problem

Fri, Feb 4, 2022

Read in 2 minutes

Psychic energy is used to help identify a problem.

Psychic Energy is used

I used psychic energy to help identify a problem. But before you jump of the rails, this is an absolutely true story.

I happened to be working in New Jersey and was asked by the New York office if I would consult with one of their engineers at a site in Long Island. I took the train over to the city and we rode out in the FA’s car.

I have to admit I felt rather bored, having done this so much. Anyway, we arrived on site.

I commune with the computer

So, I waltz in. The customer is there. The computer is there. I pull up a chair next to the computer and begin to commune with it. To an outsider, it looks like I’m meditating, but I’m not. Essentially, you open your mind to the collective sub-conscience and you first ask for the answer and then you listen.

Thirty seconds go by, and dead silence in the room. By this time, the FA and the customer were over in a corner talking with each other. The customer asks the FA what I am doing. I say ‘Communing’.

You’ve got to understand we are fifteen miles out of the city - New York City - the land of the NYC minute. It’s a tough place, NYC, if you are not used to the culture there.

So, to break the awkward silence, the FA speaks up. “You know, I put a patch in last week, and come to think of it, that’s when the problem started.”

The Solution Becomes Obvious

Light bulbs go off.

The FA takes the patch out, and the problem is resolved.

The customer thanks us and I return to the city with the FA. Later, I take the train back to Jersey.

I was on-site for a total of fifteen minutes.

Cudos from my manager

The next day, I get a glowing thank you from my manager.

Lessons Learned

  1. I have learned that whenever I walk into a problem, the answer is there already, but can’t be seen. All I have to do is bring it to consciousness. Don’t be disrespectful of anyone. Listen. Draw them out. The problem and solution will appear.
  2. Whether you believe in psychic-energy or not, I’ve used it time and time again. I know it’s real.
